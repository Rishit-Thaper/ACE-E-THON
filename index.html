About this Event

ACE-a-THON a National Level Hackathon, is going to be organized by CodroidHub Private Limited in association with Ambala College of Engineering and Applied Research, Ambala (Haryana), India during the last week of November. ACE-a-THON is an event where programmers collaborate intensively with one another and sometimes with people in other specialties to create code, usually for a new software product or service. It's a fantastic opportunity for developers, Hackers and Technology enthusiasts to develop innovative solutions to the challenges in society, industry and the environment.

Event Stages

ROUND-I: 19-21 November, 2022

  • Teams must get register themselves via this link
  • Teams have to choose one SDG from list and give their theme for selected SDG.
  • The registered teams are required to submit their PPT in the defined format by 21st November, 2022.

ROUND-II The Finale: 29th November, 2022

  • Round-II will take place in physical manner.
  • Teams that qualified Round-I will compete in Round-II.
  • Round-II will be of 24 hours.
  • In Round-II teams will have to create the prototype as well as to present their solutions to a panel of experts.

ACE-a-THON Rules

  • Individuals are allowed to participate as long as they are currently pursuing education in any Indian university/college.
  • This is a team-based competition. Each team must have 3-4 members. There should be at least one female candidate in the team.
  • Interested teams are required to register themselves online via this link before 21st November 2022, 11:59 pm. All of the members will be required to register, and only the team leader has to submit the deliverables.

Themes

SDG based Themes

The 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015, provides a shared blueprint for peace and prosperity for people and the planet, now and into the future. At its heart are the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), which are an urgent call for action by all countries - developed and developing - in a global partnership.
